"TU RATA IDEAL"

A quirky comedy visual novel

"Tu Rata Ideal" is a quirky comedy-visual novel that parodies the genre itself. You'll meet a zany cast of characters with opportunities to interact and build unique friendships. Domestic street, snow, or however you like it—embark on wild adventures in the bustling city of Santiago, where nothing is quite as it seems.

In the magical realm of Eldoria, a group of gifted children uncovers an ancient prophecy of approaching darkness. Guided by a wise mentor, they embark on a quest through enchanted lands, facing mythical challenges to learn the true power of friendship and unity.

PROJECT ESCAPE

An urban short film of art and rebellion

Set on the vibrant urban streets, follow an intrepid girl as she escapes the police on her skateboard, leaving behind a trail of ephemeral art. This short film captures the essence of freedom, creativity, and rebellion in every trick and stroke, blending dynamic action with artistic expression.
